Money Heist

There actually isn't a movie called Money Heist. It's a Spanish heist crime drama series ​​known as La Casa de Papel in Spanish. The series follows a mastermind named the Professor who recruits a group of people for an ambitious heist at the Royal Mint of Spain. With hostages taken and the police on edge, they print billions of euros in an elaborate plan. The story is full of twists and has interesting characters, making it a popular international hit.
